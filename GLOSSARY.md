## Key Processes

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Bootstrap** | Session start: load documents, confirm state, surface open items |
| **Handoff** | Session end: summary, decisions, insights, archive transitions, next focus |
| **Confirmation before capture** | Verify before committing to persistent documents |

## Core Principles

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Curated persistence** | Capture what matters, discard what doesn't, archive what might |
| **Pollution** | Noise in documents. Expensive. Anti-pattern. |
| **Distill wisely** | Essence over volume |
